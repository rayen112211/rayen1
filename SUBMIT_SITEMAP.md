# Google Search Console - Sitemap Submission

## Quick Action Required (2 minutes)

Since you've already verified your domain in Google Search Console, here's how to submit your sitemap:

### Steps:
1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console/sitemaps?resource_id=sc-domain:rayenlazizi.tech
2. In the "Add a new sitemap" field, enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click **Submit**

That's it! Google will start crawling your site immediately.

### What to expect:
- **Status will show "Success"** within a few minutes
- **Indexed pages** will appear in 24-48 hours
- You can track progress in the "Coverage" report
